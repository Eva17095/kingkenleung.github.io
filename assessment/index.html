# Assignment 1 - Read

If you think the word read only refers to the act of looking at printed words or symbols and understand what they mean, YOU ARE WRONG!

Thanks to our superb educational system, we have been trained to associate the word read with tests, exams and winning the reading award and getting the book coupons.

What does the word "read" mean in each of the sentence below?

  1. "How about a drink, dude?" "Ah, you read my mind!"
  2. "Do you read me?" "I read you loud and clear."
Answer

To Know and Understand

  • Do you read me?
  • read sb's mind

That's it. To read is simply to know something new and to understand how something works. It is not scary at all, is it?

During the holiday, I would like you to read three materials - a video, an article and an infographic. They are all related to technology. You are recommeneded to read one every two days. When you read, I want you to focus on understanding the ideas and gaining knowledge about the world.

In order to make sure that you have read the materials, you are required to complete some simple MC questions after reading each material.

# Material 1 - the Birth of iPad (Jan 27, 2010)

His name is Steve Jobs

The old man talking on the stage is no ordinary being. He is a crazy and old genius who has invented the first up-to-scratch smartphone on Earth. It is not an exaggeration to say that everyone has a smartphone now because of him.
