# Use the conditionals to show the conditions

Everything has a cause or a trigger. Therefore, we need the conditionals to help us express such relationship between events. We use the conditionals to show that something may happen under certain conditions.

Example 1:

If you freeze water, it turns into ice.
The cause/trigger The result of the cause/trigger
The condition The result of the condition
Name: if clause Name: main clause

# The form of type 1 conditionals

Example 2:

It's 6am on Monday morning, and Margart is getting ready for work. She has to hurry because if she is late for work, her boss will be angry.

What is the condition in the above conditional statement?

if she is late for work.

What tense has been used for the verb in the if-clause?

present tense

What tense has been used for the verb in the main-clause?

future tense
